Oats

Description

You know Oats is everyone's favourite healthy breakfast dish

This mouthwatering recipe is sure to bring you back to this page a few more times when you create a craving for it

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Oats Masala/Sugar
  • Apples
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Boil Oats in water
  2. Add Oats Masala/Sugar
  3. Cook for 5 minutes and add milk to it
  4. Garnish with Apple slices and Serve Hot!
