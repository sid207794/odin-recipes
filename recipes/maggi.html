Maggi

Description

You know Maggi is everyone's favourite breakfast dish

This mouthwatering recipe is sure to bring you back to this page a few more times when you create a craving for it

Ingredients

  • Maggi noodles
  • Maggi Masala
  • Mustard Oil
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Boil Maggi in water
  2. Add Maggi Masala
  3. Cook for 5 minutes
  4. Serve Hot!
