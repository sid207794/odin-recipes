Biryani

Description

Marinated Meat cooked in Earthen handi with the famous Indian "Dum" technique along with layering of aromatic Basmati rice and nearly every spice known to man

This mouthwatering recipe is sure to bring you back to this page a few more times when you create a craving for it

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Basmati rice
  • Mustard Oil
  • Spices
  • Ghee
  • Rose Water
  • Saffron
  • Curd

Steps

  1. Marinate the Meat in curd and spices and let it sit for 24 hours.
  2. Add Basmati rice to boiling water, adding spices alongwith the rice and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat the oil in the Earthenware and and place the marinated meat at the bottom.
  4. Divide the boiled rice in three quarters and layer the first on top of the meat.
  5. Add cilantro and ghee then add another layer of rice followed by another in the same way.
  6. Seal the Earthen Pot and let it cook for 15 minutes at low flame and 30-45 minutes at high flame.
  7. Serve with raita
